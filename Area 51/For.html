Roswell New Mexico UFO Case

In July of 1947, the remains of an unknown flying object were discovered on a ranch in Roswell New Mexico. It was said by reporters that it seemed to be the shrapnel from a "flying disk", and the material seemed to be unknown to everyone. The remains were taken by the governemnt for further investigation, and it is said that they brouht it back to area 51 for testing and diagnostics. The government never really explained what it was or what happened to it, and they only indicated that it could have been a surveillance balloon. However, many thoerists and several others have come forward to state that unknown life forms were taken from the scene of the crash.
