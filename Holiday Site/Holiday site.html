What is Christmas?

Christmas is a holiday celebrated on December 25th every year. The holiday began due to its religious significance: in the Christian belief, it is the day in which Jesus was born. Today, the custom of Christmas is comprised of several things. One is present-giving. Friendsand family gather together on Christmas day to exchange gifts bought prior to that day and wrapped ad placed by the Christmas tree.
